About

Started solo. Stayed honest about it.

Switchback Studio is run by one person, working from Nepal, for clients anywhere in the world. There's no agency behind it — just a clear process and tools that make a single person genuinely capable of doing this well.

Why "Switchback"

A switchback is the zigzag path a trail takes up a steep slope — short, manageable stretches instead of one impossible climb straight up. That's the idea behind the studio: most small businesses don't need a full marketing department, they need a steady, repeatable way to keep showing up online. One batch of content at a time.

Who this is for

Small business owners, solo founders, coaches, and shop owners who know they should be posting regularly and don't have the time, the words, or the patience to do it themselves — but still want it to sound like them, not like a template.

How the work actually gets made

AI drafts it. A person checks it.

  • Step 1

    Your brief — your business, your audience, your tone — goes in as the starting point for every piece of content, not a generic prompt.

  • Step 2

    AI tools draft captions, scripts, and calendar structures quickly, which is what keeps prices low without cutting corners on volume.

  • Step 3

    Every draft is read and edited by hand before delivery — checked against your brief, trimmed of anything generic-sounding, and formatted to post.

A note on being based in Nepal

Worth saying plainly

Working with a studio based in Nepal doesn't change what you receive — the deliverables, deadlines, and quality bar are the same as anywhere else. It does mean payment happens through Fiverr or a direct invoice rather than some processors that aren't available here, which is noted on the contact page so there are no surprises.
