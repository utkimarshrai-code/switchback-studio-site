Privacy policy

What happens to your information.

Last updated: June 2026. Plain-language summary below — replace the business name and links with your own before publishing.

What this site collects

This site collects information only when you choose to give it: your name and email if you submit the contact form, and your email address if you sign up for the Trailhead Pack. This site does not use tracking cookies beyond basic, anonymous analytics to understand which pages are visited.

How it's used

Contact form submissions are used only to reply to your enquiry and discuss a possible project. Email signups are used only to send the requested download and occasional updates about Switchback Studio. Your information is never sold, rented, or shared with advertisers.

Third-party tools used to run this site

  • Hosting

    This site is hosted by a static hosting provider (for example, Netlify), which may log basic technical data such as IP address for security purposes.

  • Forms

    Contact form submissions are processed by Formspree, a third-party form service, and delivered by email.

  • Email

    Email signups are stored and managed by MailerLite, a third-party email marketing service, in order to send the requested content.

Each of these providers has its own privacy policy governing how they handle data on their platforms.

Your choices

You can unsubscribe from any email at any time using the link at the bottom of each message. To request that your contact form or email data be deleted, send a request to the email address on the contact page.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated as the tools used to run this site change. The "last updated" date above will reflect the most recent revision.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to the email address listed on the Contact page.

This page is a starting template, not legal advice. If your business grows, handles payment details directly, or serves users in regions with specific requirements (such as the EU's GDPR), have the policy reviewed properly.
